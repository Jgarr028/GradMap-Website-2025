Problem Description

College students and their advisor often spend a lot of time trying to figure out timing conflicts when preparing to register for classes. Required classes may sometimes overlap with each other or students having other commitments. When a required class is only offered at certain times, students may get stuck and unable to register which can lead to a delay in graduation.


Solution

GradMap is a web app that connects to a schoolâ€™s course catalog and registration system. Students start by entering their degree/major, preferred time blocks and constraints. The app auto builds a valid plan and gives alternatives. The app will also notate when a class is necessary to take that semester.
