Elevator Pitch

Gradmap is a web app that automatically builds personalized, conflict-free class schedules for students. It considers degree requirements, course availability, and personal preferences, then shares the plan with advisors for quick review and approval. By combining planning, scheduling, and advisor collaboration in one place, Gradmap saves time and keeps students on track to graduate.

Problem Description

College students and their advisors often spend a lot of time trying to figure out timing conflicts when preparing to register for classes. Required classes may sometimes overlap with each other or students having other commitments. When a required class is only offered at certain times, students may get stuck and unable to register, which can lead to a delay in graduation.

Customer and End User

Gradmap is designed primarily for college students, both undergraduate and graduate, who need an easier, smarter way to plan their classes. These students often face challenges balancing degree requirements, limited course offerings, and personal schedules. Gradmap simplifies that process by automatically generating optimized, conflict-free schedules tailored to each student's academic path and preferences. This includes pre-requisites and co-requisites. The secondary users are academic advisors, who use the platform to review, approve, and adjust student schedules efficiently. All together, Gradmap enhances communication between student and advisors, reduces any scheduling errors that can occur, and ensures students graduate on time.
